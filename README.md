# 🌍 TravelMind AI

> **Plan Less. Experience More.**

TravelMind AI is an AI-powered travel planning platform that transforms the way people discover, plan, and experience journeys. It combines intelligent recommendations, immersive UI, and interactive visualizations to generate personalized travel itineraries tailored to each user's preferences, budget, and travel style.

Designed with a premium user experience inspired by Apple, Linear, Airbnb, and Stripe, TravelMind AI makes travel planning effortless, engaging, and enjoyable.

## 🚀 Adaptive Journey Simulator

TravelMind AI allows users to simulate real-world travel situations such as:

- Flight delays
- Bad weather
- Budget changes
- Hotel cancellation
- Local festivals
- Emergency situations

The AI instantly rebuilds the itinerary with updated schedules, costs, and recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Planning a trip often requires switching between multiple websites for flights, hotels, weather updates, maps, visa information, budgets, and local attractions. This fragmented process leads to decision fatigue, wasted time, and poorly optimized travel experiences.

TravelMind AI solves this by bringing every essential travel planning tool into one intelligent platform.

---

## 💡 Solution

TravelMind AI acts as an intelligent travel operating system that understands the user's:

- Budget
- Destination
- Travel Duration
- Interests
- Food Preferences
- Adventure Level
- Weather Preferences
- Accessibility Requirements

Using AI, it generates a complete personalized travel experience rather than just a simple itinerary.
